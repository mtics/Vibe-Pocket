## codex-micro-4-core2

Vibe Pocket's experimental Codex Micro compatibility work uses protocol
observations and HID identity details documented by
[imliubo/codex-micro-4-core2](https://github.com/imliubo/codex-micro-4-core2)
at revision `2ee23a4ab696f94bb78d250f28cc4a9b879ba079`.

The research also compared independently published observations and behavior
from the pinned sources recorded in `CODEX_MICRO_RESEARCH.md`, including
Patchself/CodexMacro, PlaneshiftDev/microd, gantrol/AgentController,
mpociot/codex-micro-stream-deck-emulator, JimLiu/decode-codex,
VoiceFlowTeam/codex-faces-bridge, xuruiray/Stopwatch-Micro,
shirok1/xiaomiao-embassy, and several USB or host-side adapters. No source file
from those additional projects is included here. Projects without an applicable
